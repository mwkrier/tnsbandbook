The Next Step: Band Method - Compiled by MWKrier


What is The Next Step you ask? This is a beginning band method book that is not out to reinvent the wheel but to refocus it. On occasion through out a school year, we as directors ask "Why doesn't my band sound better?". Personally, I asked myself that numerous times. What could I have been doing wrong? I had good size bands, they played at their appropriate skill levels, and the long tones were an abundant part of my routine. I was also using the same band method book I was taught on and it worked for me.

Several factors made me stop and rethink the what and how in my teaching strategies.

First, a colleague of mine introduced me to the concept of small instruments, a book by Eddie Green, and the realization that the only important thing to accomplish as a beginner is proper tone. Performing long tones is good practice but if we do not continually correct the bad and lazy habits of the improper emboucheres in our band no amount of long tones can fix that.

Second, the method book was not in line with how we need to teach students. There are numerous concepts being thrown at the students in every section of the book. I relate it to a firehose; the books opens a fire hose on the students but only a small percentage of the water is sticking to them.

Third, and the simplest realization, musicality needs to be practiced daily.


Why The Next Step?


  • This book allows the director to have 100%% influence on the beginning set-up with no outside information.
  • Creates a way to incorporate small instrument concept into the normal everyday routine.
  • Provides a workbook for those beginning concepts again allowing the director total control of the concepts meaning and use.
  • Eliminates all articulation and dynamics in the book. Allows the director to concertrate on tone and creatively add in musicality
  • Offers as few concepts at a time in each section. Once that concept is mastered you can move on to the Next Step
  • Virtual Books for each instrument that are mobile responsive
  • This is all free use what you think will help!
